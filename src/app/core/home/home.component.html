Who We Are

A service for Lehigh University students that helps connect tutors with tutees taking courses in the Computer Science and Engineering department.

Scroll down to learn more about how to find/become a tutor!

Becoming a Tutor

It takes less than 60 seconds to become a tutor by filling out our simple form. When a tutee is interested in your tutoring services, you will receive an email, via your Lehigh account, from that student. You keep 100% of the money made.

Finding a Tutor

Be able to see all available tutors before you commit to one. You can select a tutor based on criteria that matter to you. Once you find the perfect tutor, just click "Email Tutor" to set up a session.
