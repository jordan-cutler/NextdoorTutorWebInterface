What Is NextdoorTutor?

A group of Lehigh University students helping to connect our peers with tutoring services in the Computer Science and Engineering department at Lehigh.

Sign up today to find or become a tutor!


Create an Account

It Takes Less Than 60 Seconds to Become a Tutor

All you need to do is create an account with NextdoorTutor, fill out a simple form to set your rate and the course you want to tutor, then you're done!

Select the Tutor You Want

When finding a tutor for a course, you will get a list of tutors with their picture, rate, grade, and any other notes the tutor included. When you find the perfect tutor, simply select "contact", then create a personalized message to send to your future tutor!

It's Completely Free to Use!

NextdoorTutor doesn't charge fees to any user of the site! Tutors keep 100% of their money. We don't charge students to find a tutor, either.
